Our Equipment Fleet

Roadcon Construction maintains a modern fleet of heavy construction equipment to ensure efficient project execution and high-quality results. Our machinery is regularly serviced and maintained to the highest standards, ensuring optimal performance and reliability on every job site.

With our extensive equipment inventory, we have the capacity to handle projects of various sizes, from small municipal road improvements to large-scale highway construction. Our fleet includes:

Maintenance & Support

All equipment undergoes rigorous preventive maintenance schedules to ensure peak performance and minimize downtime. Our maintenance facility includes:

  • Full-service workshop with diagnostic capabilities
  • Welding and fabrication shop for component repair
  • Parts inventory for major equipment brands
  • Trained mechanics and service technicians
  • 24/7 emergency repair service for critical projects

Our equipment management system tracks usage, maintenance intervals, and performance metrics to optimize fleet efficiency and ensure regulatory compliance.
