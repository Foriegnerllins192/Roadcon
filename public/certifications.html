Compliance Policies

Roadcon Construction maintains comprehensive policies to ensure compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards:

Key Compliance Areas:

  • Labor Laws: Full compliance with Ghana Labour Act and fair employment practices
  • Tax Compliance: Current with all tax obligations and filings
  • Insurance Coverage: Comprehensive liability and workers' compensation insurance
  • Environmental Protection: Adherence to EPA regulations and sustainable practices
  • Anti-Corruption: Zero tolerance policy for bribery and corruption
  • Data Protection: Compliance with Data Protection Act 2012 (Act 843)

All employees receive regular training on compliance requirements and our code of conduct. We maintain detailed records of all certifications, training, and compliance activities to ensure continued adherence to standards.
