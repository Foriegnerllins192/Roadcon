Featured Projects

Accra-Kumasi Highway Expansion

2023 Greater Accra

Expansion of the major highway connecting Accra to Kumasi, increasing capacity from 2 to 4 lanes over a 45km stretch. This project improved travel times and reduced congestion on this vital economic corridor.

Completed Highway

Tema Metropolitan Roads

2022 Greater Accra

Development of urban road network in Tema to support growing metropolitan needs. Included construction of 15km of new roads, installation of drainage systems, and street lighting.

Completed Urban Roads

Northern Region Rural Access

2021 Northern Region

Construction of 80km of rural access roads to improve connectivity in remote communities. The project included 15 bridges and culverts to ensure year-round accessibility during the rainy season.

Completed Rural Roads

Koforidua Bypass

2023 Eastern Region

Ongoing construction of a 12km bypass to reduce traffic congestion in Koforidua. The project includes a 300-meter bridge over the Oti River and modern interchanges for improved traffic flow.

Ongoing Bypass

Cape Coast Drainage System

2022 Central Region

Installation of a comprehensive drainage system in Cape Coast to prevent flooding during heavy rains. The project included 15km of underground drains and 25 drainage outlets.

Completed Drainage

Sunyani Commercial Park

2023 Bono Region

Development of infrastructure for a new commercial park including 5km of access roads, parking for 500 vehicles, and utility connections. The project is scheduled for completion in early 2024.
